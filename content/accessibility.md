At Pike & West, we believe that art and celebration should be accessible to everyone. Our commitment to accessibility extends from our physical venue to our digital presence, ensuring that all guests can fully experience what makes our space special.

## Our Venue Accessibility

Pike & West welcomes guests of all abilities to our Germantown gallery and event venue. We have designed our space with inclusivity in mind.

**Physical Access:**

- Ground-level entrance with no steps required
- Wide doorways throughout the venue
- Accessible parking spaces near the entrance
- ADA-compliant restroom facilities
- Open floor plan with flexible furniture arrangements

**Event Accommodations:**

- Reserved seating areas for guests with mobility needs
- Flexible event layouts to accommodate wheelchairs and mobility devices
- Quiet spaces available upon request for guests who need a break from stimulation
- Service animals welcome

If you have specific accessibility needs for your event, please [contact us](/contact/) in advance. We work with each client to ensure every guest feels comfortable and included.

## Digital Accessibility

We are committed to making our website accessible to people with disabilities and continuously improving the experience for all users.

### Our Standards

We aim to comply with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Our ongoing efforts include:

**Navigation and Structure:**

- Keyboard navigation support throughout the site
- Skip-to-content links for screen reader users
- Consistent, predictable navigation across all pages
- Clear heading hierarchy for easy content scanning

**Visual Design:**

- Color contrast ratios that meet WCAG AA requirements
- Text that remains readable when enlarged up to 200%
- Alternative text for all meaningful images
- No content that relies solely on color to convey information

**Content and Interaction:**

- Semantic HTML structure for assistive technology compatibility
- Descriptive link text that makes sense out of context
- Form labels and error messages that are clear and helpful
- No time limits on content interaction
