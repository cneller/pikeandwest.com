Pike & West is more than an event venue. We are a working art gallery dedicated to showcasing exceptional artwork from local and regional artists. Our rotating exhibitions create a distinctive atmosphere that sets our space apart and enriches every celebration hosted within our walls.

## Exhibition Opportunities

We welcome applications from artists working in a variety of mediums, including painting, sculpture, photography, mixed media, and installation. Our gallery space features:

- High ceilings and abundant natural light
- Flexible display configurations
- Professional lighting systems
- Climate-controlled environment

## What We Look For

Our curatorial approach favors work that complements the sophisticated, minimalist aesthetic of our venue while making a bold artistic statement. We are particularly drawn to pieces that:

- Demonstrate technical excellence and originality
- Engage viewers across diverse backgrounds
- Create conversation and connection
- Enhance the experience of events held in our space

## Application Process

Complete the application form below to submit your work for consideration. Please be prepared to share:

- Artist statement and biography
- Portfolio of 10-15 representative works
- Proposed exhibition concept (optional)
- Availability for a 3-6 month exhibition period

Our team reviews applications on a rolling basis. Artists selected for exhibition will be contacted within 4-6 weeks of submission.

## Questions?

For inquiries about our gallery program, exhibition scheduling, or partnership opportunities, please [contact us](/contact/) directly or call 901.206.5575.

We look forward to discovering your work and exploring how it might become part of the Pike & West experience.
