At Pike & West, we believe creativity lives in everyone. Our [gallery space](/about/) transforms into an inspiring workshop studio where you can explore new artistic skills in a welcoming, supportive environment.

## Why Choose Pike & West Workshops

Our creative workshops offer more than just instruction. Set within our art gallery in the heart of Germantown, each session immerses you in an atmosphere designed to spark inspiration. Whether you are picking up a paintbrush for the first time or refining techniques you have practiced for years, you will find a place here.

### What Makes Our Workshops Special

- **Gallery Setting**: Create surrounded by rotating exhibitions from local and regional artists
- **Small Class Sizes**: Personal attention from instructors in intimate groups
- **All Materials Included**: Canvas, paints, brushes, aprons, and everything you need
- **Take Home Your Art**: Leave with a finished piece you created yourself
- **Welcoming Community**: Connect with fellow creatives in a relaxed atmosphere

## Workshop Types

We offer a variety of creative experiences throughout the year:

- **Painting Workshops**: Acrylics, watercolors, and mixed media on canvas
- **Pottery Sessions**: Hand-building and wheel-throwing basics
- **Seasonal Specials**: Holiday-themed projects and collaborative events
- **Private Group Sessions**: Perfect for team building or celebrations

## Perfect for Any Occasion

Our workshops make memorable experiences for:

- **Date Nights**: Create together in an inspiring setting
- **Girls' Night Out**: Bond over brushes and beverages
- **Team Building**: Spark creativity and collaboration with your colleagues
- **Birthday Celebrations**: A unique way to celebrate milestones
- **Solo Exploration**: Treat yourself to dedicated creative time
