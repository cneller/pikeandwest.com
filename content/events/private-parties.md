### The Pike & West Difference

What sets our private event venue apart from traditional Memphis-area options?

**Art That Inspires**: Our gallery walls feature rotating exhibitions from regional and national artists. This creates built-in conversation starters for your guests and ensures your celebration has a backdrop that photographs beautifully and feels genuinely special.

**Intimate Scale, Flexible Space**: Our venue accommodates gatherings from 20 to 150 guests. The open floor plan adapts from seated dinners to cocktail receptions to dancing, allowing the evening to flow naturally.

**Central Germantown Location**: Easy access from Memphis, Collierville, and surrounding areas. Ample on-site parking means your guests arrive relaxed and ready to celebrate.
