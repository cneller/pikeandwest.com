## A Beautiful Beginning Deserves a Beautiful Celebration

Every new arrival deserves a celebration as special as the journey ahead. At Pike & West, baby showers unfold against a backdrop of rotating art exhibitions, creating an atmosphere that feels both sophisticated and warmly intimate. Our Germantown gallery space offers Memphis-area families a distinctive setting that transforms traditional showers into memorable experiences.

Whether you envision an elegant afternoon tea, a modern co-ed celebration, or an intimate gathering of your closest friends and family, Pike & West provides the refined canvas your occasion deserves.

### Why Pike & West for Your Baby Shower

What sets our baby shower venue apart from traditional Memphis party spaces?

**An Inspiring Atmosphere**: Our gallery walls feature curated artwork that rotates throughout the year. The contemporary aesthetic creates natural conversation starters and provides stunning photo backdrops that feel effortlessly elevated.

**Warm, Welcoming Ambiance**: Unlike sterile event halls, our space feels like an extension of home. Comfortable seating arrangements, soft lighting, and thoughtful details create an environment where guests relax and connect.

**Flexible Configurations**: Our open floor plan adapts from intimate gatherings to larger celebrations. Need space for games, a gift-opening area, and a dessert station? We can accommodate seamless flow throughout your event.

**Climate-Controlled Comfort**: Year-round temperature control ensures your guests remain comfortable regardless of season. No more worrying about summer heat or winter chill affecting your special day.

### Baby Shower Styles We Host

We welcome baby showers of every style, each tailored to the parents-to-be's personality:

- **Traditional afternoon tea showers** with elegant finger foods and refined touches
- **Co-ed "baby-q" celebrations** that bring both sides of the family together
- **Gender reveal parties** with space for that memorable moment
- **Sprinkle showers** for second-time parents wanting a fresh celebration
- **Themed celebrations** from boho-chic to garden party to honey bee
- **Intimate gatherings or larger parties** of up to 60 guests

### Space and Capacity

Our gallery accommodates baby showers of various sizes:

- **Intimate showers**: 15-30 guests with comfortable lounge-style seating
- **Medium gatherings**: 30-50 guests with mixed seating and standing areas
- **Larger celebrations**: Up to 60 guests with flexible room configurations

The versatile layout allows for multiple activity zones, including dedicated areas for games, gifts, refreshments, and photos within a single event.
