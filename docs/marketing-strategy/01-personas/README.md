# Pike & West Customer Personas

> Understanding our ideal customers to drive targeted marketing and exceptional experiences.

## Overview

Pike & West serves affluent families in the Memphis suburbs (Germantown, Collierville, East Memphis) seeking intimate, upscale venues for milestone celebrations. Our personas reflect the women who typically research, plan, and book these events.

| Attribute          | Profile                                          |

| **Decision Maker** | Women, 28-55 years old                           |
| **Capacity Need**  | 25-125 guests                                    |
| **Event Style**    | Upscale, intimate, memorable                     |

## Cultural Insights

Pike & West serves a diverse community. Key cultural considerations:

- **African American families** in the Germantown/Collierville area often celebrate milestones with larger gatherings and more elaborate celebrations
- **Multi-generational events** are common, requiring space that accommodates grandparents through young children
- **Photo documentation** is extremely important - events must be "Instagram-worthy"
- **Catering flexibility** matters - many families have preferred caterers or family members who cook

## Common Threads Across Personas

### Shared Values

- Quality over quantity
- Creating lasting memories
- Impressing guests
- Supporting local businesses
- Unique experiences over cookie-cutter venues

### Shared Pain Points

- Fear of the unknown (never hosted at a venue before)
- Budget anxiety (wanting upscale but watching costs)
- Vendor coordination stress
- Time pressure from busy schedules

### Universal Decision Factors

1. Visual appeal (photos/videos of past events)
2. Flexibility (timing, catering, decor)
3. Responsiveness of venue staff
4. Clear, transparent pricing
5. Social proof (reviews, testimonials)
