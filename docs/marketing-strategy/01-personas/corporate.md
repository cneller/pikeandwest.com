## 4. Pain Points & Challenges

### External Challenges

- Finding unique venues that aren't hotels or restaurants
- Working within corporate budget approval processes
- Coordinating with multiple internal stakeholders
- Managing headcount fluctuations
- Accommodating dietary restrictions at scale
- AV/tech requirements for presentations
- Venue availability for popular corporate dates (December, Q4)

### Internal Challenges

- Pressure to outdo last year's event
- Fear of choosing a venue that's "too different"
- Stress of managing vendor relationships
- Worry about accessibility or accommodation gaps
- Budget justification to finance/leadership
- Time pressure (managing events on top of regular duties)

### Objections (Why She Might NOT Book)

- "Is an art gallery professional enough for our brand?"
- "Can it handle our AV/presentation needs?"
- "What's the catering situation? We need reliable corporate catering."
- "Is there adequate parking for 75+ people?"
- "How does this compare cost-wise to our usual hotel?"
- "Will executives feel it's appropriate?"

### Past Bad Experiences

- Hotel ballroom that felt generic and forgettable
- Restaurant that couldn't handle the group size well
- Venue with inadequate AV that embarrassed presenters
- Caterer that ran out of food
- Space that was too small/crowded
- Venue with hidden fees that blew budget

## 8. Decision-Making Process

### Who Else Is Involved

- **CFO/Finance** - Budget approval
- **CEO/Executive team** - Final sign-off on larger events
- **HR** - Input on employee events
- **Marketing** - Brand alignment concerns
- **Administrative assistant** - May handle logistics

### Timeline from Search to Booking

| Phase             | Duration  | Activities                            |
|-------------------|-----------|---------------------------------------|
| Research          | 2-4 weeks | Venue searches, initial inquiries     |
| RFP process       | 1-2 weeks | Formal requests for proposals         |
| Site visits       | 2-3 weeks | Tour shortlisted venues               |
| Internal approval | 1-2 weeks | Budget approval, stakeholder sign-off |
| Negotiation       | 1 week    | Finalize terms, contract review       |
| Booking           | 3-5 days  | Legal review, signature, deposit      |

### Key Decision Criteria

1. **Professionalism** - Does it reflect well on the company?
2. **Capacity/Layout** - Does it work for our event type?
3. **Cost** - Within budget with clear pricing?
4. **Location** - Convenient for attendees?
5. **Flexibility** - Can it accommodate our specific needs?
6. **Reliability** - Track record of successful corporate events?

### Dealbreakers

- Unprofessional communication or slow response
- Lack of corporate event experience/examples
- Inadequate AV capabilities
- Poor accessibility
- Hidden fees or unclear pricing
- Inflexible policies
- Negative reviews mentioning corporate events
