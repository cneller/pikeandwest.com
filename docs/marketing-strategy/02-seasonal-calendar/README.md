# Seasonal Calendar & Content Planning

> Strategic timing guide for Pike & West's marketing content based on persona planning behaviors and event seasonality.

## Planning Lead Times by Persona

Understanding when each persona begins their venue search is critical for content timing.

| Persona     | Typical Lead Time | When They Search                 | When Event Happens   |
|-------------|-------------------|----------------------------------|----------------------|
| corporate   | 3-12 months       | Jan-Mar for year, Aug-Oct for Q4 | Year-round, heavy Q4 |
| anniversary | 2-4 months        | 2-4 months before date           | Year-round           |
| birthday    | 1-3 months        | 1-3 months before                | Year-round           |
| graduation  | 1-3 months        | Feb-May                          | May-June             |
| prom        | 2-4 weeks         | Mar-Apr                          | Apr-May              |
| baby-shower | 1-2 months        | 1-2 months before                | Year-round           |

## Annual Marketing Rhythm

Month-by-month breakdown of event focus, content themes, priority personas, and promotional opportunities.

### January

- **Event Focus:** Corporate planning season, New Year celebrations
- **Content Themes:** "Start the year right," corporate event planning guides, venue booking tips for the year ahead
- **Priority Personas:** Corporate planners (annual planning), anniversary couples (Valentine's prep)
- **Promo Opportunities:** Early booking incentives for corporate clients, Valentine's Day packages

### February

- **Event Focus:** Valentine's Day, early spring planning
- **Content Themes:** Romantic venue features, anniversary celebration ideas, graduation venue search begins
- **Priority Personas:** Anniversary couples, graduation families (starting search)
- **Promo Opportunities:** Valentine's promotions, spring booking incentives

### March

- **Event Focus:** Spring events, prom season begins, graduation planning intensifies
- **Content Themes:** Spring event inspiration, prom venue guides, graduation party planning
- **Priority Personas:** Prom planners, graduation families, baby shower hosts
- **Promo Opportunities:** Prom packages, spring event specials

### April

- **Event Focus:** Peak prom bookings, graduation confirmations, spring corporate events
- **Content Themes:** Last-minute prom tips, graduation countdown content, spring celebration galleries
- **Priority Personas:** Prom planners (urgent), graduation families, corporate (Q2 events)
- **Promo Opportunities:** Limited availability urgency messaging

### May

- **Event Focus:** Graduation parties, Mother's Day, late spring celebrations
- **Content Themes:** Graduation success stories, summer event planning, Mother's Day brunch ideas
- **Priority Personas:** Graduation families (executing), summer birthday planners, baby shower hosts
- **Promo Opportunities:** Summer booking incentives, graduation packages

### June

- **Event Focus:** Summer events begin, Father's Day, wedding season adjacent
- **Content Themes:** Summer celebration inspiration, corporate summer gatherings, milestone birthdays
- **Priority Personas:** Birthday planners, corporate (summer retreats), anniversary couples
- **Promo Opportunities:** Summer event packages, weekday specials

### July

- **Event Focus:** Peak summer events, Independence Day, summer birthdays
- **Content Themes:** Summer party galleries, outdoor event tips (if applicable), mid-year corporate events
- **Priority Personas:** Birthday planners, corporate, baby shower hosts
- **Promo Opportunities:** Beat-the-heat indoor venue messaging, summer specials

### August

- **Event Focus:** Back-to-school, Q4 corporate planning begins, late summer celebrations
- **Content Themes:** Fall event previews, corporate year-end planning guides, back-to-school celebrations
- **Priority Personas:** Corporate planners (Q4 focus), birthday planners, anniversary couples
- **Promo Opportunities:** Early Q4 corporate booking incentives

### September

- **Event Focus:** Fall season begins, corporate Q4 intensifies, fall birthdays
- **Content Themes:** Fall event inspiration, holiday party planning (early), anniversary celebration ideas
- **Priority Personas:** Corporate planners (Q4), anniversary couples, birthday planners
- **Promo Opportunities:** Fall booking specials, holiday party early-bird rates

### October

- **Event Focus:** Halloween events, heavy Q4 corporate booking, fall celebrations
- **Content Themes:** Halloween party inspiration, corporate holiday party guides, fall celebration galleries
- **Priority Personas:** Corporate planners (urgent Q4), birthday planners, baby shower hosts
- **Promo Opportunities:** Holiday party packages, Halloween event specials

### November

- **Event Focus:** Thanksgiving, holiday party season begins, gratitude events
- **Content Themes:** Holiday gathering inspiration, year-end celebration guides, Thanksgiving content
- **Priority Personas:** Corporate planners (holiday parties), anniversary couples, birthday planners
- **Promo Opportunities:** Black Friday/holiday booking specials, January booking incentives

### December

- **Event Focus:** Peak holiday parties, year-end celebrations, New Year's Eve
- **Content Themes:** Holiday party execution tips, year-in-review content, New Year planning
- **Priority Personas:** Corporate planners (executing), anniversary couples (New Year's), birthday planners
- **Promo Opportunities:** New Year's packages, January/Q1 early booking incentives

## Content Cadence

Recommended posting frequency and optimal timing for each channel.

| Channel                 | Frequency       | Best Days/Times        |
|-------------------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Blog                    | 2-4 posts/month | Tue-Thu                |
| Instagram               | 3-5 posts/week  | Tue-Thu evenings 7-9pm |
| Facebook                | 3-4 posts/week  | Wed-Fri afternoons     |
| Google Business Profile | 1-2 posts/week  | Weekdays               |
| Email Newsletter        | 1-2/month       | Tue or Thu morning     |

### Channel-Specific Notes

**Blog:**

- Long-form content (1,000-2,000 words) for SEO
- Focus on evergreen guides and seasonal inspiration
- Include strong CTAs for tour booking

**Instagram:**

- Visual storytelling: event photos, venue details, behind-the-scenes
- Use Stories daily during events
- Reels for venue tours and event highlights

**Facebook:**

- Mix of original content and Instagram cross-posts
- Event announcements and availability updates
- Community engagement and local business connections

**Google Business Profile:**

- Updates about availability, seasonal offerings
- Photos from recent events
- Respond to all reviews promptly

**Email Newsletter:**

- Curated content from blog and social
- Exclusive offers for subscribers
- Event spotlights and testimonials
