# Local SEO Checklist - Pike & West

> Comprehensive local SEO implementation for dominating Germantown/Memphis event venue searches.

## Why Local SEO is Critical

For a venue business, **80%+ of qualified traffic comes from local searches**. Google prioritizes local results for "near me" and location-based queries. A complete local SEO strategy ensures Pike & West appears in:

- Google Maps results
- Local Pack (3-pack) on search results
- "Near me" searches
- Voice search results

### Citation Consistency Requirements

**NAP Format (use exactly across all listings):**

```text
Pike & West
2277 West Street
Germantown, TN 38138
(901) 206-5575
```

**Common Variations to Avoid:**

- "Pike and West" (use ampersand)
- "2277 W Street" (spell out "West")
- "Germantown, Tennessee" (use "TN")
- "901.206.5575" (use parentheses and hyphen)

## Review Management

### Review Solicitation Strategy

**When to Ask:**

- Immediately after successful event
- Follow-up email 3-5 days post-event
- At the end of planning process (even before event)

**How to Ask:**

1. Personal email from event coordinator
2. Include direct link to Google review
3. Make it easy (provide link, not just instructions)
4. Thank them regardless of outcome

**Review Request Email Template:**

```text
Subject: How was your experience at Pike & West?

Hi [Name],

We loved hosting your [event type] at Pike & West! Seeing
[personal detail] was such a highlight for our team.

If you have a moment, we'd be grateful if you could share
your experience on Google. Your feedback helps other
families and couples find us:

[Direct Google Review Link]

Thank you for choosing Pike & West for your special day!

Warm regards,
[Coordinator Name]
Pike & West
```

### Review Response Guidelines

**Positive Reviews (4-5 stars):**

- [ ] Respond within 24 hours
- [ ] Thank them by name
- [ ] Reference specific event details
- [ ] Mention something personal
- [ ] Keep it warm and genuine

**Example:**

> Thank you so much, Sarah! We loved hosting your anniversary celebration - the surprise on your parents' faces was priceless. Moments like these remind us why we love what we do. We hope to see your family again soon!

**Negative Reviews (1-3 stars):**

- [ ] Respond within 24 hours
- [ ] Apologize for their experience
- [ ] Take conversation offline
- [ ] Don't be defensive
- [ ] Offer to make it right

**Example:**

> We're sorry to hear your experience didn't meet expectations. This isn't the standard we hold ourselves to. Please reach out directly to [email] so we can understand what happened and make it right. - [Name], Owner
