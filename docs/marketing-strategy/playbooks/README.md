# Marketing Playbooks

Step-by-step guides for Pike & West's marketing operations. Each playbook is designed to be followed without additional context.

## Available Playbooks

| Playbook                                            | Purpose                                           | When to Use                      |
|-----------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| [Writing a Blog Post](./writing-a-blog-post.md)     | End-to-end blog creation workflow                 | Weekly content creation          |
| [Posting to Social](./posting-to-social.md)         | Content distribution across channels              | After each blog post or event    |
| [Responding to Reviews](./responding-to-reviews.md) | Templates and best practices for review responses | Within 24-48 hours of new review |
| [Asking for Reviews](./asking-for-reviews.md)       | Review solicitation strategy                      | 1-3 days after each event        |

## Playbook Overview

### Content Creation

**Writing a Blog Post** - The complete workflow from topic selection to publication. Covers outline creation, drafting, editing, adding images, and generating social content. Use weekly to maintain consistent content output.

### Content Distribution

**Posting to Social** - How to distribute blog content across Instagram, Facebook, and Google Business Profile. Includes scheduling guidance and UTM tracking.

### Reputation Management

**Responding to Reviews** - Templates and guidelines for responding to both positive and negative reviews. Includes what to say, what NOT to say, and response timing.

**Asking for Reviews** - Systematic approach to soliciting reviews after events. Covers email templates, in-person scripts, QR codes, and follow-up sequences.

## Workflow Integration

These playbooks work together in a typical content cycle:

1. **Weekly**: Follow [Writing a Blog Post](./writing-a-blog-post.md) to create new content
2. **Post-publish**: Follow [Posting to Social](./posting-to-social.md) to distribute
3. **Post-event**: Follow [Asking for Reviews](./asking-for-reviews.md) within 1-3 days
4. **Ongoing**: Follow [Responding to Reviews](./responding-to-reviews.md) within 24-48 hours of any new review
