## Photo Strategy

### Photo Categories

Organize uploads into these categories:

| Category | Content                      | Upload Frequency |
|----------|------------------------------|------------------|
| Interior | Venue spaces, rooms, details | Monthly          |
| Exterior | Building, entrance, parking  | Quarterly        |
| At work  | Events in progress, setup    | After each event |
| Team     | Staff photos                 | Annually         |
| Identity | Logo, branding               | As needed        |

### Photo Guidelines

- Minimum resolution: 720 x 720 pixels
- Preferred: 1200 x 900 pixels or larger
- Format: JPG or PNG
- File size: Under 5MB
- Orientation: Landscape preferred for main photos

### Photo Upload Schedule

**After Every Event:**

- 5-10 best photos from the event
- Focus on decor, setup, venue shots
- Include variety of spaces used
- No identifiable guests without permission

**Monthly:**

- 2-3 new venue/interior shots
- Seasonal decoration updates
- Art installation changes

**Quarterly:**

- Exterior shots (different seasons)
- Team photos if updated
- Professional photography refresh

### Photo Optimization

- Add descriptive alt text/captions
- Include location in filename (pike-west-germantown-wedding.jpg)
- Showcase different event types
- Feature unique venue elements
- Maintain consistent quality and style

## Review Management

### Review Request Strategy

**When to ask:**

- 1-3 days after successful event
- After positive verbal feedback
- Following completed venue tours
- When client expresses satisfaction

**How to ask:**

```text
Thank you so much for celebrating with us at Pike & West! We truly enjoyed being part of your special day.

If you have a moment, we'd be grateful if you'd share your experience on Google. Your feedback helps other families find us and means so much to our team.

[Direct link to Google review]

Thank you again for choosing Pike & West!
```

### Response Time Standards

| Review Type     | Response Time   |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| 5-star positive | Within 48 hours |
| 4-star positive | Within 48 hours |
| 3-star neutral  | Within 24 hours |
| 2-star negative | Within 24 hours |
| 1-star negative | Within 12 hours |

### Response Templates

**5-Star Review:**

```text
Thank you so much for this wonderful review, [Name]! We absolutely loved being part of your [event type] and creating those special memories with you. Your kind words mean the world to our team. We hope to see you again for future celebrations!
```

**4-Star Review:**

```text
Thank you for taking the time to share your experience, [Name]! We're so glad you enjoyed your [event type] at Pike & West. We appreciate your feedback and are always looking for ways to make every celebration even more special. Thank you for choosing us!
```

**3-Star Review:**

```text
Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We're glad you chose Pike & West for your event, and we appreciate you sharing your thoughts. We'd love to hear more about how we can improve. Please feel free to reach out to us directly at [email] so we can discuss your experience further.
```

**Negative Review (2-star or below):**

```text
Thank you for bringing this to our attention, [Name]. We're sorry to hear your experience didn't meet expectations, and we take this feedback seriously.

We'd like to understand more about what happened and how we can make it right. Please contact us directly at [phone] or [email] so we can discuss this personally.

Thank you for giving us the opportunity to address your concerns.
```

### Handling Negative Reviews

**Do:**

- Respond promptly and professionally
- Acknowledge the concern
- Apologize for the experience (not necessarily fault)
- Take conversation offline
- Follow up after resolution
- Learn and improve processes

**Don't:**

- Delete or ignore negative reviews
- Get defensive or argumentative
- Share private details publicly
- Make excuses
- Blame the customer
- Respond emotionally
