## Content Mix

### Weekly Posting Schedule

| Day       | Content Type                 | Notes                       |
|-----------|------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Monday    | Venue shot or art feature    | Start week with inspiration |
| Tuesday   | Event photo (recent)         | Optimal engagement day      |
| Wednesday | Stories focus (5+ stories)   | Mid-week engagement boost   |
| Thursday  | Event photo or carousel      | Peak engagement day         |
| Friday    | Reel or behind-scenes        | Weekend planning mode       |
| Saturday  | Stories (if event happening) | Real-time event coverage    |
| Sunday    | Rest or light story content  | Optional engagement         |

### Content Categories & Frequency

#### Event Photos (2-3x per week)

Primary content showcasing celebrations at Pike & West.

- Feature recent weddings, parties, corporate events
- Focus on decor details, table settings, floral arrangements
- Include candid moments (with permission)
- Tag vendors when appropriate
- Use natural lighting whenever possible

**Caption formula:**

```text
[Opening hook - emotion or detail]
[Event description - 1-2 sentences]
[Vendor credits - photographers, florists, etc.]
[Call to action]
[Hashtags - in first comment or at end]
```

#### Venue Shots (1x per week)

Showcase the space itself.

- Different rooms and angles
- Seasonal decor changes
- Art installation updates
- Golden hour lighting
- Empty venue "potential" shots

#### Stories (3-5x per day during events, daily otherwise)

- Behind-the-scenes setup
- Real-time event glimpses
- Polls and questions
- Countdown to events
- Reposts from tagged content
- Quick tips and inspiration

#### Reels (1-2x per week)

High-impact video content.

- 15-30 seconds optimal
- Use trending audio when appropriate
- Event highlight montages
- Venue tours
- Setup time-lapses
- Before/after transformations

## Story Strategies

### Daily Story Framework

1. **Morning** (8-10 AM): Inspirational quote or venue beauty shot
2. **Midday** (12-2 PM): Behind-scenes or educational tip
3. **Evening** (6-8 PM): Engagement prompt (poll, question, quiz)

### Story Content Ideas

**Engagement-Focused:**

- "This or That" polls (decor options, color schemes)
- Question stickers ("What's your dream event theme?")
- Quizzes about venue facts
- Countdown to open houses or events
- Slider reactions on beautiful setups

**Value-Focused:**

- Planning tips carousel
- Vendor spotlights
- "Did you know?" venue facts
- Seasonal planning reminders
- Trend updates

**Behind-the-Scenes:**

- Event setup time-lapses
- Staff introductions
- Delivery day excitement
- Decor transformations
- Post-event breakdown

### Story Highlight Management

- Update highlights monthly
- Remove outdated content
- Keep 5-10 stories per highlight
- Use consistent cover icons (match brand colors)
- Feature newest content prominently

## Reel Ideas

### High-Performing Reel Concepts

1. **Venue Tour** (30-60 sec)
   - Walk through each space
   - Highlight unique features
   - End with call to action

2. **Event Transformation** (15-30 sec)
   - Empty room â†’ fully decorated
   - Time-lapse setup
   - Before/after split screen

3. **Day-of-Event Montage** (15-30 sec)
   - Quick cuts of preparation
   - Guest arrivals
   - Celebration moments
   - Use trending audio

4. **Planning Tips Series** (15-30 sec)
   - "3 things to consider when choosing a venue"
   - "What to ask on your venue tour"
   - "Timeline for booking your event venue"

5. **Seasonal Inspiration** (15-30 sec)
   - Holiday decor ideas
   - Seasonal color palettes
   - Trending themes for the season

6. **Vendor Collaborations** (15-30 sec)
   - Feature photographer, florist, caterer
   - Cross-promote to their audiences

### Reel Best Practices

- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Use captions (many watch without sound)
- Include clear call to action
- Post at peak times
- Engage with comments immediately
- Share to Stories for extra reach

## Engagement Tactics

### Response Strategy

- Respond to all comments within 24 hours
- Like and respond to DMs within 4 hours
- Use personal, warm tone (not corporate)
- Ask follow-up questions to continue conversation
- Thank people for shares and tags

### Proactive Engagement

**Daily (15-20 minutes):**

- Engage with local venue hashtags
- Comment on tagged photos
- Interact with vendor partners' content
- Respond to Stories mentions

**Weekly:**

- Follow new local wedding/event accounts
- Engage with Memphis/Germantown community accounts
- Connect with local influencers
- Share user-generated content (with permission)

### User-Generated Content

- Create branded hashtag for guests
- Request permission before reposting
- Credit original creator always
- Feature in Stories and occasional feed posts
- Build relationships with frequent taggers

## Analytics & Optimization

### Key Metrics to Track

- Reach and impressions
- Engagement rate (likes + comments + saves / followers)
- Story completion rate
- Reel views and shares
- Profile visits
- Website clicks
- DM inquiries

### Monthly Review Process

1. Export Insights data
2. Identify top 5 performing posts
3. Analyze what made them successful
4. Note worst performing content
5. Adjust strategy accordingly
6. Update hashtag strategy based on reach data
7. Test new posting times if needed
