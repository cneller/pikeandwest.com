# Social Media Strategy

> Platform-specific marketing strategies for Pike & West's social media presence.

## Platform Overview

| Platform                | Role                    | Primary Audience         | Content Focus                                |
|-------------------------|-------------------------|--------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Instagram               | Inspiration & Discovery | Younger planners (25-45) | Event photos, venue shots, Reels             |
| Facebook                | Community & Trust       | Older planners (40-60)   | Event recaps, testimonials, local engagement |
| Google Business Profile | Conversion & Local SEO  | High-intent searchers    | Updates, photos, Q&A                         |

## Target Audience Alignment

Pike & West's primary audience consists of:

- **Middle-aged+ women** planning milestone celebrations (weddings, anniversaries, birthdays)
- **African American families** who value celebration and hosting memorable gatherings
- **Corporate event planners** seeking upscale, intimate venues in the Germantown area

Each platform serves different stages of the customer journey:

1. **Discovery** (Instagram) - Visual inspiration, dream-building
2. **Research** (Facebook, GBP) - Social proof, reviews, community validation
3. **Conversion** (GBP) - Contact info, directions, booking inquiries

## Content Repurposing Flow

Maximize content efficiency by repurposing across platforms:

```text
Blog Post
    ↓
Instagram Carousel (key takeaways)
    ↓
Instagram Reel (visual summary)
    ↓
Facebook Post (full share with commentary)
    ↓
Google Business Post (condensed update)
    ↓
Email Newsletter (curated roundup)
```

### Example Workflow

A single wedding feature can become:

1. **Blog**: "Sarah & Marcus's Elegant Garden Wedding at Pike & West"
2. **IG Carousel**: 8-10 best photos with planning tips in captions
3. **IG Reel**: 15-30 second video montage with trending audio
4. **FB Post**: Full story with couple's testimonial
5. **GBP Post**: "What's New" featuring the event
6. **Email**: Monthly "Recent Celebrations" feature

## Posting Frequency Summary

| Platform          | Minimum | Optimal   | Content Type                  |
|-------------------|---------|-----------|-------------------------------|
| Instagram Feed    | 3x/week | 4-5x/week | Photos, carousels             |
| Instagram Stories | Daily   | 3-5x/day  | Behind-scenes, polls, Q&A     |
| Instagram Reels   | 1x/week | 2x/week   | Event highlights, venue tours |
| Facebook          | 3x/week | 5x/week   | Mixed content                 |
| GBP Posts         | 1x/week | 2x/week   | Updates, events, offers       |

## Brand Voice by Platform

### Instagram

- Aspirational and elegant
- Conversational but refined
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 per caption)
- Focus on "the experience"

### Facebook

- Warm and community-focused
- Celebratory and proud
- Share fuller stories
- Engage with local community

### Google Business Profile

- Professional and informative
- Keyword-conscious for SEO
- Focus on practical details
- Prompt action (book, call, visit)

## Crisis & Response Protocol

### Response Time Standards

| Platform           | Standard Inquiries | Urgent/Negative |
|--------------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| Instagram DM       | Within 4 hours     | Within 2 hours  |
| Instagram Comments | Within 24 hours    | Within 4 hours  |
| Facebook Messages  | Within 2 hours     | Within 1 hour   |
| Facebook Comments  | Within 24 hours    | Within 4 hours  |
| GBP Reviews        | Within 48 hours    | Within 24 hours |
| GBP Q&A            | Within 24 hours    | Within 4 hours  |

### Escalation Process

1. Negative comment/review identified
2. Do NOT delete (unless policy violation)
3. Acknowledge publicly within response time
4. Move conversation to private channel
5. Document issue and resolution
6. Follow up publicly if appropriate
