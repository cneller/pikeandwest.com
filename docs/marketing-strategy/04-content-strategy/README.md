# Content Strategy

> Pike & West's content approach: warm, sophisticated, and helpful guidance for planning memorable celebrations at our intimate Germantown venue.

## Content Mission

Help families and planners in the Memphis area create extraordinary milestone celebrations by providing expert guidance, local insights, and inspiration tailored to intimate gatherings.

## Content Pillars

Our content is organized into five strategic pillars that serve different audience needs while supporting our business goals.

### 1. Planning Guides (35%)

How-to content, checklists, and actionable resources that establish Pike & West as the expert in intimate event planning.

**Content types:**

- Step-by-step planning guides
- Printable checklists and timelines
- Budget worksheets and templates
- Vendor coordination guides
- Day-of logistics tips

**Example topics:**

- "The Complete Guide to Planning a 50th Birthday Party"
- "Your 8-Week Graduation Party Planning Checklist"
- "How to Coordinate Vendors for Your Anniversary Dinner"

**Goals:** Build trust, capture planning-stage searches, generate email signups

### 2. Inspiration (25%)

Ideas, themes, and visual content that sparks creativity and helps planners envision their celebration.

**Content types:**

- Theme and decor galleries
- Color palette inspiration
- Tablescape and styling ideas
- Seasonal trend roundups
- Real event spotlights

**Example topics:**

- "15 Elegant 60th Birthday Party Themes"
- "Moody Floral Tablescape Ideas for Fall Anniversaries"
- "Prom Send-Off Party Decor That Photographs Beautifully"

**Goals:** Drive Pinterest traffic, increase time on site, inspire venue inquiries

### 3. Local Expertise (20%)

Content highlighting our deep knowledge of the Memphis/Germantown vendor community and local scene.

**Content types:**

- Vendor spotlights and interviews
- Local supplier roundups
- Seasonal Memphis event guides
- Neighborhood dining and entertainment tips
- Regional tradition features

**Example topics:**

- "Top 10 Bakeries for Custom Birthday Cakes in Memphis"
- "Germantown Florists Who Specialize in Event Design"
- "Memphis Photographers Perfect for Intimate Celebrations"

**Goals:** Build local SEO, strengthen vendor relationships, establish community presence

### 4. Behind the Scenes (10%)

Content showcasing the venue, team, and what makes Pike & West unique.

**Content types:**

- Venue feature tours
- Team member spotlights
- Art gallery exhibitions
- Setup and transformation reveals
- Our story and values

**Example topics:**

- "Inside Pike & West: A Tour of Our Art Gallery Venue"
- "How We Transform the Space for Different Celebrations"
- "Meet Our Team: The People Behind Pike & West"

**Goals:** Build emotional connection, differentiate from competitors, humanize the brand

### 5. Social Proof (10%)

Testimonials, reviews, and event highlights that demonstrate real results.

**Content types:**

- Client testimonials
- Event recap features
- Before/after reveals
- Guest feedback highlights
- Award and recognition announcements

**Example topics:**

- "Sarah's Surprise 40th: An Evening of Art and Celebration"
- "What Our Clients Say About Pike & West"
- "Event Highlight: The Johnson Family Anniversary Dinner"

**Goals:** Build credibility, reduce purchase anxiety, encourage referrals

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone Attributes

- **Warm**: Friendly without being casual; we're hosting you in our home
- **Sophisticated**: Elevated language without being pretentious
- **Helpful**: Genuinely useful guidance, not sales pitches
- **Confident**: Expert authority without condescension
- **Celebratory**: Share in the joy of milestone moments

### Writing Style

- Use "you" and "your" to speak directly to readers
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Include specific, actionable advice
- Reference local landmarks and businesses by name
- Avoid jargon and overly formal language
- Write headlines that promise clear value

### Words We Use

- Intimate, elegant, sophisticated, memorable
- Milestone, celebration, gathering
- Curated, personalized, bespoke
- Memphis, Germantown, local

### Words We Avoid

- Cheap, budget, affordable (use "thoughtful" or "intentional" instead)
- Party rental, event hall, banquet room
- Generic, standard, typical
- Overused superlatives (best, amazing, incredible)

### Publishing Cadence

| Content Type            | Frequency  | Lead Time |
|-------------------------|------------|-----------|
| Blog posts              | 2-4x/month | 4-8 weeks |
| Social posts            | 4-6x/week  | 1-2 weeks |
| Email newsletter        | 2x/month   | 2-3 weeks |
| Google Business Profile | 2-3x/week  | Same week |

### Seasonal Content Planning

Content should be published based on planning lead times:

- **3-6 months before season**: High-intent planning guides
- **1-3 months before**: Inspiration and vendor roundups
- **During season**: Social proof and last-minute tips
- **After season**: Recap content and testimonials
