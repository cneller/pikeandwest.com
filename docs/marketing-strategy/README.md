# Pike & West Marketing Strategy

> A comprehensive content-driven marketing strategy to achieve 50% revenue growth through AI-assisted content creation, local SEO dominance, and targeted social media engagement.

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-20

---

## Executive Summary

Pike & West is an upscale art gallery and event venue in Germantown, TN, offering premium spaces for weddings, corporate events, milestone celebrations, and private parties. This marketing strategy aims to achieve **50% revenue growth** through:

1. **Content-Driven Awareness** - Creating valuable, searchable content that positions Pike & West as the premier event venue in the Memphis area
2. **Local SEO Dominance** - Capturing high-intent searches for "event venues Germantown," "wedding venues Memphis," and related terms
3. **AI-Assisted In-House Production** - Leveraging AI tools to create professional-quality content efficiently without a large marketing team
4. **Targeted Persona Marketing** - Speaking directly to our ideal clients through personalized messaging and content

## Strategy Overview

### Approach: AI-Assisted In-House Content Creation

Rather than relying on expensive agencies or a large marketing team, Pike & West will leverage AI tools to produce high-quality content at scale:

- **Blog Posts & SEO Content** - AI-assisted research and drafting, human editing for brand voice
- **Social Media** - AI-generated caption variations, human curation and approval
- **Email Campaigns** - Template-based automation with personalized touches
- **Visual Content** - Curated photography with AI-enhanced editing

### Target Demographics

**Primary Audience:**

- Middle-aged and older women (35-65) in the Germantown/Memphis area
- Decision-makers for family celebrations and milestone events
- Household income $100K+
- Value quality, elegance, and personalized service

**Secondary Audience:**

- African American families who celebrate milestones in a big way
- Corporate event planners and HR professionals
- Wedding planners and event coordinators

### Geographic Focus

- **Primary:** Germantown, Collierville, Memphis East
- **Secondary:** Greater Memphis metro, North Mississippi, Eastern Arkansas
- **Tertiary:** Tennessee destination events (Nashville, Chattanooga)

## Target Personas

Our marketing strategy centers on six key personas. Each has detailed documentation including pain points, messaging, content preferences, and booking journey.

### [Birthday - The Milestone Mom](./01-personas/birthday-milestone-mom.md)

The family celebration coordinator planning significant birthdays (Sweet 16, 50th, milestone birthdays). She wants an elegant venue that impresses guests and creates lasting memories.

**Key Motivators:** Making loved ones feel special, photo-worthy moments, stress-free planning

### [Anniversary - The Anniversary Celebrator](./01-personas/anniversary-celebrator.md)

Planning a significant anniversary celebration (25th, 50th, or vow renewal). Values sophistication, meaningful touches, and honoring a lifetime of love.

**Key Motivators:** Romance, elegance, celebrating a legacy, family involvement

### [Prom - The Prom Parent](./01-personas/prom-parent.md)

Parent organizing a pre-prom gathering or after-party. Wants a safe, elegant space that creates Instagram-worthy moments for their teen's milestone.

**Key Motivators:** Safety, sophistication, memorable photos, teen approval

### [Corporate - The Corporate Event Planner](./01-personas/corporate-event-planner.md)

HR professional or executive assistant planning company events (holiday parties, team retreats, client appreciation). Needs professional ambiance with reliable service.

**Key Motivators:** Impressing leadership, seamless execution, budget accountability

### [Graduation - The Graduation Host](./01-personas/graduation-host.md)

Parent celebrating a child's educational achievement (high school, college, professional degree). Wants to honor years of hard work with an elegant celebration.

**Key Motivators:** Pride, accomplishment recognition, family gathering space

### [Baby Shower - The Baby Shower Organizer](./01-personas/baby-shower-organizer.md)

Friend or family member planning a memorable baby shower. Seeks an elevated experience beyond typical backyard or restaurant gatherings.

**Key Motivators:** Honoring the mom-to-be, unique experience, beautiful photos

## Content Pillars

All content aligns with five strategic pillars that support our positioning and SEO goals:

### 1. Planning Guides

Comprehensive, actionable content that helps prospects plan their events while positioning Pike & West as an expert resource.

**Examples:**

- "The Complete Guide to Planning a 50th Birthday Party in Memphis"
- "Corporate Event Timeline: 12 Weeks to a Perfect Company Celebration"
- "What to Look for When Touring Event Venues in Germantown"

### 2. Inspiration

Visual and aspirational content showcasing possibilities at Pike & West.

**Examples:**

- Event galleries organized by type and style
- Decor and theme ideas for each event category
- Color palette and seasonal styling guides

### 3. Local Expertise

Content that demonstrates deep knowledge of the Germantown/Memphis event ecosystem.

**Examples:**

- Vendor spotlight features (caterers, photographers, florists)
- "Best of Memphis" roundups related to events
- Local event trends and seasonal considerations

### 4. Behind the Scenes

Authentic content that builds trust and showcases the Pike & West experience.

**Examples:**

- Event setup time-lapses
- Team member spotlights
- Venue features and hidden details

### 5. Social Proof

Testimonials, reviews, and real event stories that validate the Pike & West experience.

**Examples:**

- Client testimonial videos and quotes
- Real event recaps with permission
- Awards, features, and media mentions

## Marketing Channels

### Instagram (Inspiration & Discovery)

**Purpose:** Visual storytelling, brand awareness, reaching new audiences

**Content Mix:**

- 60% Event photos and venue shots
- 20% Behind-the-scenes and team content
- 15% Client testimonials and real events
- 5% Promotional (booking CTAs)

**Posting Frequency:** 4-5 posts/week, daily Stories

**Key Metrics:** Follower growth, engagement rate, profile visits, link clicks

### Facebook (Community & Trust)

**Purpose:** Community building, detailed information, parent demographic reach

**Content Mix:**

- 40% Event photos and albums
- 25% Blog post shares and articles
- 20% Client stories and testimonials
- 15% Local community content

**Posting Frequency:** 3-4 posts/week

**Key Metrics:** Page likes, post reach, event responses, message inquiries

### Google Business Profile (Conversion & Local SEO)

**Purpose:** Local search visibility, review collection, direct bookings

**Content Focus:**

- Regular photo uploads
- Prompt review responses
- Q&A management
- Google Posts for events and offers

**Update Frequency:** Weekly photo additions, immediate review responses

**Key Metrics:** Profile views, direction requests, calls, website clicks, review count/rating

### Blog (SEO & Authority)

**Purpose:** Organic search traffic, expertise demonstration, lead generation

**Content Types:**

- Long-form planning guides (1,500-2,500 words)
- Local expertise articles (800-1,200 words)
- Event recaps and inspiration (600-1,000 words)

**Publishing Frequency:** 2-4 posts/month

**Key Metrics:** Organic traffic, keyword rankings, time on page, conversion to inquiry

## How to Use This Strategy

### Quick Start: Creating Content

1. **Identify your content goal** - SEO, social engagement, or lead nurturing?
2. **Choose your persona** - Who is this content for? Reference [01-personas/](./01-personas/)
3. **Select your content pillar** - Planning guide, inspiration, local expertise, behind-scenes, or social proof?
4. **Check the seasonal calendar** - Is this timely? Reference [02-seasonal-calendar/](./02-seasonal-calendar/)
5. **Draft using AI assistance** - Use the templates in [playbooks/content-creation.md](./playbooks/content-creation.md)
6. **Optimize for SEO** - Apply keywords from [03-seo-strategy/](./03-seo-strategy/)
7. **Add tracking** - Include UTM parameters per [06-utm-tracking/](./06-utm-tracking/)

### Quick Start: Posting to Social

1. **Review the content calendar** - What's scheduled for this week?
2. **Select platform** - Instagram for visual inspiration, Facebook for community
3. **Use templates** - Reference [05-social-media/content-templates.md](./05-social-media/content-templates.md)
4. **Add tracking links** - UTM parameters for any external links
5. **Schedule or post** - Use scheduling workflow in [08-automation/social-scheduling.md](./08-automation/social-scheduling.md)
6. **Engage with responses** - Monitor and respond within 24 hours

### Quick Start: Tracking Performance

1. **Weekly check** - Review [07-measurement/kpi-dashboard.md](./07-measurement/kpi-dashboard.md) metrics
2. **Monthly review** - Full performance analysis per [07-measurement/reporting-cadence.md](./07-measurement/reporting-cadence.md)
3. **Quarterly strategy adjustment** - Update targets and tactics based on data
